Privacy Policy

Trilayer: 3D Block Puzzle

Last updated: June 26, 2026

Overview

Trilayer is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information.

Data We Collect

Trilayer uses TelemetryDeck, a privacy-first analytics service, to collect anonymous usage data:

App launch and session duration
Game mode played and scores achieved
Feature usage (e.g., camera rotation, sharing)
Device type and OS version (anonymized)
App language setting

TelemetryDeck does not use cookies, does not track users across apps, and does not collect IP addresses. All data is aggregated and anonymous. Learn more at telemetrydeck.com/privacy.

Data We Do NOT Collect

No names, email addresses, or contact information
No location data
No advertising identifiers
No cross-app tracking
No data sold to third parties

Game Center

Trilayer supports Apple Game Center for leaderboards, managed entirely by Apple under Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

Trilayer does not knowingly collect any data from children.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
